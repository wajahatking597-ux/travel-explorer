Islamabad & Gilgit are most demanded destinations by our customers.

Islamabad

Islamabad, the capital city of Pakistan, is known for its modern architecture, lush greenery, and scenic views. Key attractions include the Faisal Mosque, Daman-e-Koh, and the Pakistan Monument. The city is a perfect mix of natural beauty and urban charm, offering scenic hiking trails and peaceful surroundings.

Gilgit

Gilgit, the gateway to the mighty Karakoram Range, offers breathtaking mountains, crystal rivers, and adventure. It serves as a hub for trekkers and mountaineers heading to K2 and Nanga Parbat, with scenic valleys and the iconic Karakoram Highway.

Skardu

Swat Valley

Explore More, Worry Less

Karachi

Karachi, the city of lights, is a vibrant metropolis known for its beaches, markets, and cultural diversity. Experience Clifton Beach, Quaid’s Mausoleum, and the city’s lively food and nightlife scene.

Faisalabad

Faisalabad, known as the Manchester of Pakistan, is a textile hub full of energy and culture. Visit Ghanta Ghar, Jinnah Garden, and the lively bazaars that define this city’s spirit.

Lahore

Lahore, the cultural heart of Pakistan, is rich with Mughal heritage. Explore Badshahi Mosque, Lahore Fort, and indulge in its world-famous food and festivals.

Multan

Multan, the city of saints, is known for its shrines, bazaars, and crafts. Visit Multan Fort and experience the city’s deep spiritual and cultural charm.

Peshawar

Peshawar, a city of history and tradition, is famous for Bala Hisar Fort, Qissa Khwani Bazaar, and its unique food. A perfect blend of past and present.

Quetta

Quetta, the fruit garden of Pakistan, offers mountainous beauty, Hanna Lake, and vibrant bazaars. A blend of tribal culture and natural beauty.

Murree

Murree, the Queen of Hills, is a serene hill station surrounded by pine forests and misty mountains — a perfect escape for peace and beauty.
