Explore & Learn — Travel Quiz

Test your knowledge about Pakistan’s most amazing travel destinations!

Hunza Valley is located in the Gilgit-Baltistan region of northern Pakistan, known for its majestic mountains and friendly people.

Skardu is known for its stunning lakes like Shangrila and Satpara, and for being the gateway to K2 and other high peaks.

The Thar Desert, located in Sindh, is famous for its cultural heritage and archaeological sites near Mohenjo-Daro.

Quetta is known for its delicious dry fruits, beautiful Hanna Lake, and the scenic Ziarat Valley nearby.

Karachi is called the City of Lights — it’s Pakistan’s largest city, full of life, beaches, and vibrant culture.

Lahore is known for its Mughal architecture, delicious food, and historic sites like Badshahi Mosque and Lahore Fort.
